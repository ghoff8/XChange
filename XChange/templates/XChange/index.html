XChange




Welcome to XChange, a stock and cryptocurrency purchasing simulator. XChange is here to help you understand more about investing with no risk! Our website provides real time prices of companies as well as some features to build your confidence in stocks and cryptocurrency.



XChange™ is a one-stop finance monitoring website aimed at people who are new to investing, without requirements of huge funds or monthly minimums. The recent rise in cryptocurrency and ease of quick-investing apps such as Robinhood or Acorn has led to greater opportunities for people to invest. However these apps do not provide knowledge or experience to people that are new to investing. We aim to provide that chance for knowledge and experience with XChange. Our goal is to provide an environment for users to try out the selling and buying ofstocks and cryptocurrencies to gain knowledge and experience, with no risk. This will be done by giving each new user a certain amount of virtual money that they can use to start investing. We’ll use the IEX™ and IEX Cloud™ APIs to access stock and coin prices. We’ll create investment portfolios for each user to show them their earnings as well as a breakdown of each stock and coin they have invested in. Our application will feature access to real-time stock and cryptocurrency prices. We believe that through simulated buying and selling, as well as learning to monitor your investment portfolio, knowledge and experience is gained. This lets people that are new to investing, notice and learn patterns to the market.
